The Best Movies According to William

My top 3 movies of all-time.

John Wick

There is a lot of action in this movie, and plots which makes you shocked throughout the movie.

The Imitation Game

This movie takes us back to world war 2, where a secret group of smart people try to
come up with a solution to decrypt the messages when germany communicated with eachother.

Bohemian Rhapsody

This movie is a master piece. They show us the history of the band Queen, and the
devestating life of Freddie Mercury.
